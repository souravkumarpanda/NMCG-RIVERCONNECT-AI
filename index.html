The National Mission for Clean Ganga (NMCG) is an ambitious initiative launched by the Indian government to rejuvenate and clean the Ganga River. The mission aims to tackle pollution, enhance riverfront development, and promote sustainable water management practices. NMCG's comprehensive approach includes developing sewage treatment infrastructure, conducting river surface cleaning, implementing afforestation projects, and raising public awareness. The program also emphasizes the importance of biodiversity and strives to transform rural areas along the river through the Ganga Gram initiative.
